### Ground Transportation:

Transportation from BNA Nashville Airport to Vanderbilt University Area:
- [BNA airport website on Ground transportation](https://flynashville.com/ground-transportation)
- Rideshare (Uber & Lyft)
  - Estimated Cost: $20–$35 (varies by demand)
  - Pickup Location: Follow airport signs to the designated rideshare pickup area outside of baggage claim.
  - Time to Destination: 15–20 minutes-
- Taxi Services
  - Estimated Cost: $30–$40 (flat-rate fares may apply)
  - Pickup Location: Taxi stand outside baggage claim
  - Time to Destination: 15–20 minutes
  - Best For: Quick access, no app required
- Rental Cars
  - Estimated Cost: Varies by rental company
  - Pickup Location: Rental car facility near the terminal (shuttle service available)
  - Time to Destination: 15–20 minutes
  - Best For: Those planning to traveling beyond Nashville
